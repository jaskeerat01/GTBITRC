Why Publish
with IJTIS?

We prioritize technically correct, unique, and strictly peer-reviewed manuscripts.

IJTIS publishes articles appealing to readers across various fields. As a growing open-access platform, IJTIS provides wide accessibility to research findings, enabling broad dissemination of knowledge. Our double-blind process ensures that research is vetted holistically on merit alone.

Disclaimer

Only original and previously unpublished research papers are accepted. Manuscript submission implies certification that no part of the manuscript is copyrighted by another publication.

Paper Acceptance Requirements

Following are the prerequisites for an article to be considered for evaluation:

Format & Originality

Written in the IJTIS template. Must be a unique piece that adds to existing knowledge. Reported results cannot be published elsewhere.

Technical Standards

Experiments, statistics, and analyses should be detailed. High technical fidelity is required. Conclusions must be supported by data.

Visuals & Equations

Figures must be logically named. Equations made via editor (no scans). Tables in MS Word formatting strictly.

The Review Cycle

All submitted papers are initially reviewed for suitability and plagiarism using Turnitin/Plagiarism CheckerX. Selected papers undergo a double-blind peer review by at least two referees testing originality, clarity, completeness, and contribution to research. Process typically takes 5 to 15 days.
